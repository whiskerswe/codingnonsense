A King and a Queen were seated very still upon their throne.
They looked at Alice as though they were waiting for something she had already failed to do.
She assumed that sitting so quietly must require a great deal of authority.
At the sound of a buisine, the air itself seemed to shift.
The court, the royals included, were carried away by the sudden wind.
