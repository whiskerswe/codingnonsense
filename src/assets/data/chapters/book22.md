Long, shiny whiskers shone through the foliage. The cat smiled at Alice as though it expected a treat.  

"Have you forgotten everything I have done for you?", the it purred.  

Alice looked back, but said nothing. The cat frowned, and Alice found herself no longer inside the place she had been.
