The air was buzzing with industry as the Cards painted the roses black.  

“Black as spades they are.”  

“As they must be,” said another, “just as His Flyness prefers.”  

They stood back to admire their work, though each knew perfectly 
well that Le Comte preferred nothing of the kind.  

You may not be accustomed to seeing cards painting roses black; 
but then, very little proceeds in an accustomed manner in this part of the world. 
And in any case, roses do not grow upon trees, but upon shrubs, which is quite a different matter.  

“Have they caught her yet, the intruder?” asked the Five of Spades.  

No one knew, and no one was particularly concerned to know. 
When one is a playing-card, one’s chief anxiety is to avoid being bent at the corners.  

“Unless one is caught first,” said the Seven.  

But nothing further came of that.
