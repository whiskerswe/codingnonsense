Alice found herself among a group of birds, all of whom appeared to be waiting.

They regarded her politely, but without curiosity.

She thought that if she remained still long enough, something would begin.

Nothing among the birds suggested that this was the case.

Alice looked around, disappointed to find that no one seemed ready to move.
