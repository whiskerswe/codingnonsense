A Rabbit hurried past her, clutching something small and important.
He spoke aloud to no one in particular and did not slow his pace.
She concluded that whatever he was late for must already be underway.

This seemed only to make his hurry more urgent.

Alice watched him as he disappeared behind the tall grass
