Alice saw a Lizard suddenly propelled from a chimney, trailing smoke behind it.
She could not tell whether this was intentional or merely overdue.
She supposed that someone must have planned it that way.

No one appeared ready to take responsibility.
Alice stepped aside to avoid the smoke as it drifted across the ground.
