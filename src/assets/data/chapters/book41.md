A whole pack of cards rose up into the air, and came flying down upon
her: she gave a little scream, half of fright and half of anger, and
tried to beat them off. This only seemed to make them more agitated. There was no sister, and no queen to help her now. She regretted her unwise words!  

“I didn’t mean it!” pleaded the poor girl. “But you’re so easily offended, you know!”  

Alice made for the door. The cards stayed in the room, as they never fly out of doors unless someone has thrown them.
