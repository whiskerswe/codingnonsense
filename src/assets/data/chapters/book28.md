A large crowd had gathered. Alice joined it, wishing to see what was the matter.
After some time, it became apparent that they were looking at her.
She turned, and found herself face to face with the Queen.  

“Did you eat your porridge?” her Majesty enquired.   

Alice confessed that she had not.  

“Then will you be my daughter?”  

Alice reflected that she already possessed both a mother and a father, though she had not seen them for some time. It seemed possible that this country required a fresh supply of parents.
The Queen took her hesitation for consent.  

“You are my child, then. Off to your bed!”  

Being perfectly satisfied that she had discharged her parental duty, the Queen and her court departed, leaving Alice behind.
