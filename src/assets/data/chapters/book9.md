A Mouse waved its arms to call for attention, and addressed Alice and the other animals with great seriousness. Its explanation appeared to require a familiarity with events she could not recall.

She assumed that careful attention would eventually make his meaning clear.
The words continued without becoming more helpful.
Alice nodded politely, though she could not say what she had agreed to.
