Though there were no cliffs to dive from, Alice found herself already in the water, which felt perfectly familiar, though not entirely plain. She stretched her arms and swam.

A Mouse nearby regarded her with an expression suggesting it had something of importance to contribute.

If I may, Miss… One ought not to kick with one’s legs. They should ambulate vigorously.

Alice took another steady stroke, observing the Mouse with polite interest.

Feeling satisfied that it had saved yet another fellow creature from technical error, the Mouse paddled away with modest efficiency.
