Quite far away, His Right Honourable Monsieur le Comte d'Attrape-Mouches received an urgent dépêche, delivered by a page of uncommon dampness.
The letter contained alarming news of Alice’s arrival.

"Her présence in Wonderland is most irrégulière.", said le Comte. "This is a matter of constitutional extremity."  

The page was instructed to inform the king and queen at once.
He scurried off, catching a fly mid-air with admirable discretion.
