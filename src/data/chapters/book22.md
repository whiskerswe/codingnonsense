The cat's whiskers shone through the foliage.
It smiled at Alice as though she had already reached the end of something.  

"Have you forgotten everything I have done for you?", the cat purred.  

Alice looked back, but said nothing.
The cat frowned, and Alice found herself no longer inside the place she had been.
