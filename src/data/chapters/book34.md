And thus the Mock Turtle finished his tale.

‘It is a very melancholy story,’ said Alice; ‘though I cannot make out why.’

‘Why? The albatross!’ said the Mock Turtle, and wept the more.

Alice, who could not remember that any bird had been mentioned, thought it best to say nothing further; and the Mock Turtle continued to sob, as if he had lost a very particular friend.
