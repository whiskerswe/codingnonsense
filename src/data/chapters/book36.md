How do you like the tale so far?  
  

Does it divert your mind?  
  

This land will offer very little,  
  

yet take up all your time.  
  

Of princes and of princesses  

we have but little say;  

the laws of proper nonsense, though,  

insist we go this way.  


For nothing ever alters here,  

and all remains quite still,  

as clocks go on in perfect haste  

to prove they always will.  
  

My trumpet sounds — attend to it —  

though nothing is begun;  

for all our grandest enterprises  

continue ere they’re done.
