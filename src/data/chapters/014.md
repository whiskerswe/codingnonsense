Alice found herself facing a small dog that appeared deeply alarmed by her presence.

Its trembling suggested danger, though she could not tell to whom.

She assumed that moving more gently would make her less frightening.

The dog shrank back as if gentleness were beside the point.

Alice stepped away, unsure which of them had been the greater threat.
